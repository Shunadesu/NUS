Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

For all orders exceeding a value of 100USD shipping is offered for free.

Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

Returns Policy

Returns will be accepted for up to 10 days of Customer's receipt or tracking number on unworn items. You, as a Customer, are obliged to inform us via email before you return the item.

Return Conditions

  • Received the wrong item
  • Item arrived not as expected (ie. damaged packaging)
  • Item had defects
  • Over delivery time
  • The shipper does not allow the goods to be inspected before payment

The returned product(s) must be in the original packaging, safely wrapped, undamaged and unworn. This means that the item(s) must be safely packed in a carton box for protection during transport, possibly the same carton used to ship to you as a customer.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information
  • Order history
  • Communication preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your orders and payments
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Send you marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Protect against fraud and unauthorized transactions

Terms & Conditions

Website Usage

By accessing and using our website, you agree to comply with and be bound by these terms and conditions.

Product Information

We have done our best to display our items as accurately as possible via our Services. Please be aware however that variations in style, color, size, shape and look may occur.

Product Personalization

When you choose to personalize a product, please be aware that personalized items cannot be returned or exchanged unless there is a manufacturing defect. Please ensure all spelling and customization details are correct before submitting your order.

Refund Policy

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the status of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, your item must be:

  • Unworn and unused
  • In the original packaging
  • With all tags attached
  • Returned within 10 days of receipt

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Personalized items (unless defective)
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items that have been worn or used
  • Items returned after the 10-day period
